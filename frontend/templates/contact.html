Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about ResumeIQ

How does ResumeIQ analyze my resume?

Our AI uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to evaluate your resume against thousands of successful examples. It identifies key skills, checks for ATS compatibility, and provides personalized optimization suggestions.

Is my resume data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and never share your personal information without your consent. You can review our Privacy Policy for more details.

How long does it take to get a response?

Our support team typically responds within 24 hours on weekdays. For premium members, we offer priority support with responses within 4 hours during business hours.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
